WHAT'S NEW

All models are equipped with the A16 Bionic single-chip system, a 48-megapixel main camera, ProMotion displays with Ceramic Shield protection and a refresh rate range of 1-120 Hz, as well as fast LPDDR5 memory. The design of the lineup has been updated, featuring two cutouts on the front panel of the smartphones.

All iPhone 14 models in the US will be sold without a SIM card tray: the manufacturer claims that the improved eSIM technology will allow users to transfer their old electronic SIM cards to the new smartphones.

The front camera has been upgraded (it is equipped with autofocus and recognizes users faster in low-light conditions), along with the main camera sensor.

The iPhone 14 Pro has been released with a new design—without the signature "notch." A new color has also been introduced in the lineup. Unlike the "regular" iPhone 14 models, the smartphone is equipped with the A16 Bionic processor, which can perform 4 trillion operations per second when processing photos.
